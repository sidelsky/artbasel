Add your valualble products on social sharing platform using Social Share For WooCommerce plugin with responsive design.

== Description ==

<p><strong>Social Share For WooCommerce</strong> extension is used to share your products on well known social platform with images.</p>

= What the plugin does =
<p>Users sharing your products on their social network profiles</p>

= How you can benefit from it: =

<ul>
	<li>You will encourage customers to share your products on social networks improving your e-commerce visibility and renown.</li>
</ul>

<p>You can configure as you need social platform in this plugin. As per social media colour design pattern with attractive design.</p>

<p>Product social share is an excellent extension for share your products on social platform. Social Share For WooCommerce is configured with WPML. Extension is support with multilanguage.</p>

= Features: =
<ul>
    <li>Add Social Share buttons to WooCommerce product page</li>
    <li>Enable or disable buttons visibility</li>
    <li>All buttons are responsive for all devices</li>
    <li>You can position the sharing buttons to 6 different places in the product page.</li>
    <li>Admin can select button icons style from 3 different variation</li>
    <li>Click & add social buttons feature</li>
    <li>Show button icons only or with Social Media name (Icons with text)</li>
    <li>Work with WordPress and WooCommerce latest version</li>
</ul>


= Highlights: =
<ul>
	<li>Support many features.</li>
	<li>Easy to install and configure</li>
	<li>Intuitive interface.</li>
</ul>


== Installation == 

1) Download the Social Share For WooCommerce Plugin.
2) Unzip the file and upload the social-share-for-woocommerce folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory using your FTP application.
3) Activate the Social Share For WooCommerce plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
